About Me

I am a seasoned systems engineer and program manager with over a decade of experience leading groundbreaking projects in the medical device industry. My passion for advancing healthcare technology has driven me to contribute to numerous successful products and innovations, all while managing cross-functional teams.

My Journey in Medical Devices

After earning degrees in Biomedical Engineering, I began my career at Mako Surgical, where I helped design groundbreaking robotic systems for orthopedic surgery. This role allowed me to combine my engineering expertise with my passion for innovation, resulting in systems now used in thousands of surgeries worldwide.

I continued to grow my career earning my degrees in Industrial & Systems Engineering and an MBA while at Auris Robotics, where I managed the capital hardware development of a revolutionary Bronchoscopic robotic system and later an Endourology platform. After Auris was acquired by Johnson & Johnson, I further honed my skills in managing FDA submissions, leading successful 510(k) clearances, and optimizing development workflows for compliance with stringent industry standards.

Today, I work at Dexcom, leading the integration of hardware and software systems for continuous glucose monitoring (CGM) technologies.
